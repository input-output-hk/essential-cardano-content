This infographic explains the situation behind the December 2025 Cardano Constitutional Committee restoration initiative.

Delegate representatives (DReps) and stake pool operators (SPOs), [vote on the governance action via Cardano GovTool](https://gov.tools/governance_actions/4dab331457b61b824bbc6ba4b9d9be4750e25c0b5dd42207aeb63c7431a6b704#0).

For more details on the process, [read this article](https://www.essentialcardano.io/article/why-spos-and-dreps-need-to-vote-on-the-constitutional-committee-restoration).

You can [track voting on AdaStat](https://adastat.net/governances/4dab331457b61b824bbc6ba4b9d9be4750e25c0b5dd42207aeb63c7431a6b70400).
