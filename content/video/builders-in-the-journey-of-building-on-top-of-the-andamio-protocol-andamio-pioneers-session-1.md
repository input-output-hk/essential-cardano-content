Yesterday, Feb 24th, was our inaugural session of _Andamio Pioneers 2026_, welcoming Cardano builders to the journey of building on top of the [Andamio](https://x.com/andamio_teams) protocol. But how do you start a Pioneers program where builders, not the technology, are the first-class citizens? For sure, by giving them first-class technology. But what else?

**Trust**: the assurance that what they are putting their effort into building on top of is not a poisoned substance that will contaminate their reputation when they create something for their own users.

and what else? **Transparency**, guiding them through the audit document that points to our findings, giving them all the resources they need to do their own due diligence, verifying whether we value them enough to take seriously what was identified by a professional audit service like _TxPipe_.

That’s the level of responsibility, care, and consideration with which we will serve them, and international brands like [FC Barcelona](https://x.com/FCBarcelona), [Syngenta](https://x.com/Syngenta), and a few more currently in the oven.

[Andamio](https://x.com/andamio_teams): We love builders ... and their users.
