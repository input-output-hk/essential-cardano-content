Across conversations with developers, maintainers, and open-source leaders, we found genuine curiosity about how blockchain can help address challenges around trust, coordination, reputation, incentives, and collaboration at scale.

What stood out was not skepticism, but thoughtful questions and a willingness to explore new approaches when connected to real-world problems.

The opportunity is significant. There is a large audience eager to understand what this technology makes possible. The key is continuing to engage, listen, and build bridges between communities.

[Andamio](https://www.andamio.io/) is proud to be part of that effort. Thanks to [Intersect MBO](https://intersectmbo.org/) and the [Cardano Foundation](https://cardanofoundation.org/) for supporting Cardano’s presence at [this event](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/open-source-summit-north-america/), a truly leading-edge environment for innovation. Special thanks to [Terence McCutcheon](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tex-oso/) for his outstanding leadership in driving this trailblazing initiative forward and positioning Cardano in spaces where real-world collaboration and adoption are shaped.
