_The Beyond MVG team successfully concludes Milestone 2, delivering a comprehensive collection of community insights and on-chain data correlation._

**Author:** The Beyond MVG team

**Summary:**

*   The Beyond MVG team has completed its second major phase, focusing on the "who" and "what" of Cardano governance by engaging directly with the people running the ecosystem.
    
*   Representative data-collection targets have been met, gathering feedback from DReps, SPOs, CC members, and community projects.
    
*   Qualitative insights from surveys, community workshops, and interviews have been merged with year-long on-chain data (Epochs 537-609) to provide a validated "State of Governance" report outline.
    

Building on the foundational research from Milestone 1, the Beyond Minimum Viable Governance (Beyond MVG) project has now completed its collection and aggregation phase. This milestone was critical for transforming our measurement framework into actionable data through direct community engagement.

To ensure total transparency, we have collated the [Workshop, Surveys, and Interviews results](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1_FWuTPjI0COb9A3PiQB_8g1U8ruAVUf5VRGSvoP2I18/edit?usp=sharing), providing a record of the community's input during this phase.

## Timeline of Activities

Milestone 2 has been formally submitted to Intersect, with all acceptance criteria verified.

### February 2026

*   **Community Workshops:** A highlight of this milestone was the series of workshops held on January 27th, 29th, 31st, and February 18th, 2026. These sessions captured nuanced, personal experiences that hard data alone cannot reflect.
    
*   **On-Chain Correlation:** We gathered data for each of the metrics laid out in the governance measurement framework.  The data maps governance behaviors over one-year to validate anecdotal feedback with technical evidence.
    
*   **Refined Report Outline:** Utilizing structured survey forms and feedback from our X Space, we have synthesized a final "State of Governance" report outline grounded in validated stakeholder priorities.

## Looking Ahead: Milestones 3 & 4

The analysis of the data gathered will be completed and published as part of Milestone 3. This will directly inform the final recommendations for the "State of Governance" report, ensuring they are data-driven and rooted in ecosystem experiences. Once the analysis is completed, the team will be hosting another set of collaborative workshops in April to share the analysis with the ecosystem to discuss and refine the top recommendations for improving Cardano’s governance.
